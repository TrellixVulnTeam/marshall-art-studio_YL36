Rule with Expressions

Rule:

If driver's age is less than 20, refer to application.

Fields:

Drivers Date of Birth: PersAutoLineBusiness.PersDriver.DriverInfo.PersonInfo.BirthDt

Approach:

Age of the driver is less than 20 if (date of birth + 20 years is after today's date). Use an expression with a dateAdd operator to add year, months and days. You can subtract years, months and days by providing negative numbers for years, months and days.

Steps to write this rule:

  1. Choose the left operand type of expression. Within an expression, choose the operand type of field and select the driver date of birth field. Choose the dateAdd operator and enter 20 for years.
