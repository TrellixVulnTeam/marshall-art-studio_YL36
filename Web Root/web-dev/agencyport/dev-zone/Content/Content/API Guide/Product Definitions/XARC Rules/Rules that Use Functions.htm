Rule:

If year built is older than 100 years, display a referral warning.

Fields:

Year Built: HomeLineBusiness.Dwell[PrincipalUnitAtRiskInd='1'].Construction.YearBuilt

Approach:

Year built is a year field and not a date field. Check whether the year built is 100 years by adding 100 years to it and comparing with the year portion of today's date. Add 100 years using the expression and 'add' operator. Extract the year portion of today's date using substring function. The condition to test here is the following:

Year built + 100 year < year portion of todayâ€™s date

Steps to write this rule:

  1. Choose the left operand type of expression and add 100 years to it.
  2. Choose the right operand as today's date variable. Choose the substring function on the advanced tab. Enter the starting position as 1 and length as 4 to extract the first 4 characters from the date.
  3. Choose the less than operator and add the appropriate message to display when the condition is met.
