My Work

When you click the My Work tab, all work items display.

You can filter work items by line of business (LOB), status and transaction type:

LOB

  • homeowners
  • automobile - personal
  • liability
  • workers compensation
  • account
  • automobile - business

Status

  • approved
  • deleted
  • transferred
  • offline
  • in-progress
  • client-declined
  • referred
  • bound
  • rejected
  • inactive
  • declined
  • active

Transaction

  • claim
  • renewal
  • quick quote
  • endorsement
  • account
  • new business

You can select one or multiple filters. When a filter is selected, a check mark displays next to the filter option and the filter item displays under the search box. The work items display based on the filter(s) selected.

If no filters are selected, Showing all work items displays under the search box and all work items display.

Filters remain, regardless of the action performed. This includes logging out. If you want to remove a filter, you must manually uncheck the check mark in the filter drop-down menu or click the X next to the item under the search box.
